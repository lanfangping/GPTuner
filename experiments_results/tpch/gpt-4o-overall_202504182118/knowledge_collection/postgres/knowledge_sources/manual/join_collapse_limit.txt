The 'join_collapse_limit' knob, typically set to match 'from_collapse_limit', determines the maximum number of items a planner will collapse an explicit JOIN construct into, with defaults allowing automatic reordering for optimal efficiency but advanced users can set it to 1 to maintain explicit join order in queries if desired, while setting it equal to or above 'geqo_threshold' may inadvertently invoke the GEQO planner.
